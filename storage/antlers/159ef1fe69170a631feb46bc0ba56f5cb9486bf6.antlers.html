Dr. Nooreen Ibrahim is a board-qualified podiatrist who serves patients in the Highland Park area with great enthusiasm. She enjoys treating patients with all types of pathologies and different age ranges, using her conservative and surgical training. 

Dr. Nooreen Ibrahim earned her Bachelors in Neuroscience from UIC. She completed her Doctorate in Podiatric Medicine and Surgery at Des Moines University in Iowa and her residency at Wheaton Franciscan/Ascension in Milwaukee, WI. 

Dr. Ibrahim has missed her home and is glad to be back practicing in Illinois. In her free time, she enjoys spending time with her family, continuing her martial arts training, and reading. She is fluent in Hindi and Urdu.

Fun Facts about Dr. Ibrahim

  • She enjoys hiking and being outdoors.

  • Traveling is one of her favorite things to do, she has a vast list of places to visit.

  • She loves cats, and recently adopted a stray cat that needed a home.

  • She has been training in martial arts for more than ten years.
